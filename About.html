About Me

I'm Shiou Hsu - a sound designer, composer based in Boston.

I've played games and instruments ever since I was a kid, and got into game audio in 2019 when a friend asked me to compose music for their game.

Since then, I've worked with multiple indie developers over the years, shipping titles from mobile to Switch and PC.

To ensure that the projects I take on can be as polished as possible, I like to dive deep into the technology and understand how the game works.

I've learned a lot about game engines (Unity, Unreal Engine), their respective programming languages, and middlewares (FMOD, Wwise) from past projects.

If you're also interested in my music works, please check out my composing website.
